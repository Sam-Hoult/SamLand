Launching [VIVIDconcept](https://vividconcept.com), my new product design house. My passion is making things, physical and digital. VIVIDconcept is one of my steps into making real physical products that solve a problem in a new way or will just add delight through quality and obsession.

Ladies and gentlemen, I introduce to you the [**ROLLUX**](https://vividconcept.co/rollux) - the first product I'm launching. It sits in the cannabis/vice space and solves a real pain point I had around using rolling machines. I've obsessed over this problem for well over a year, and things finally aligned in my career break after experimenting with other creative pursuits to start making real products. Check out [VIVIDconcept](https://vividconcept.com) for more.

There have been many learnings and many more to come, no doubt, in learning the ropes around designing, manufacturing, and delivering physical products, especially for someone who has spent a life more focused on distributing software. That includes applying for a patent, sourcing, manufacturing, packaging, branding, marketing, sales, and more.
