A quick catch-up for anyone wondering what I've been up to. I've been spreading myself across a few different things, so here's the short version with links to more.

**[VIVIDconcept & the ROLLUX](/blog/vividconcept-rollux/)** - Launched my product design house and the first product, the ROLLUX (a rolling machine I've been obsessing over for over a year). Physical products, manufacturing, patenting, and all the fun that goes with it.

**[YOUniq.gift](/blog/youniq-gift/)** - Custom items and one-of-a-kind gifts. Early stage, but it's where a lot of the "custom physical stuff" thinking is going.

**[PrintQue](/blog/printque/)** - Open source 3D printer fleet management and automation. For when you have more than one printer and need queue, routing, and visibility without vendor lock-in.

**[PickSell integration](/blog/picksell/)** - Helping a friend manage order picking when you've got thousands of SKUs in a store. Connecting orders to location data so finding stock isn't a treasure hunt.

That's the snapshot. Deeper dives live in the posts above, and there's always the [making-of for this site](/blog/making-of-samland/) and the [music video how-to](/blog/how-to-make-a-music-video/) if you want the creative side. More soon.
