A friend runs a store on the software I designed and worked on for almost 7 years [(Greenline)](https://getgreenline.co/) with thousands of SKUs. When orders come in, the real pain is **finding** the stuff, knowing where each item lives in the space without losing your mind or your margin to endless searching.

I've been working on a [**PickSell**](https://picksell.co/) integration to help with that: connecting orders to location data and workflows so picking becomes systematic instead of a treasure hunt. It's one of those "help a friend fix a real problem" projects that turns into a proper tool. Early days, but it's already making a difference for him.

More to share when it's in a state worth documenting. If you're in a similar boat (high SKU count, physical store, orders flowing in) this is the kind of problem I'm aiming to solve.
