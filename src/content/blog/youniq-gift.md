I've been working on [**YOUniq.gift**](https://youniq.gift) - Memories to Mementos. A project focused on custom items. The idea is to make it easier for people to get truly one-of-a-kind gifts and products, whether that's personalised design, small-batch runs, or something that doesn't exist off the shelf.

It's still early, but it ties into the same thread as the rest of what I've been doing: bridging physical products, creativity, and the kind of tooling that makes custom actually feasible. More to share as it develops.
